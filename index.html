ISO 20001-1:2018

Information Technology Services Management System

About ISO 20001-1:2018

ISO 20001-1:2018 specifies requirements for establishing, implementing, maintaining, and continually improving a service management system (SMS). It provides guidance for the effective delivery of IT services to meet business and customer needs.

Features & Benefits

Service Quality

Enhance the quality of IT services with a standardized framework.

Customer Satisfaction

Improve customer experience by delivering consistent IT services.

Continuous Improvement

Foster an environment of ongoing improvement and innovation.

ISO 20001-1:2018 is an international standard for establishing and maintaining a Service Management System (SMS) for IT services.
